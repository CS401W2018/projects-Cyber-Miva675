MARTIN GOODMAN

Martin. Moe. Goodman, born January 18, 1908 was an American publisher of magazines, and comic books, who founded Timely Comics in 1939, which was later named Marvel Comics. Goodman was the founder and publisher of Marvel Comics till he passed away at June 6, 1992.

KEVIN FIEGE

Kevin Fiege, born June 2, 1973, is an American television and film producer. He is the primary producer of the MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe) and the President of the Marvel Studios today since 2007.

STAN LEE

Stanley Martin Lieber, born December 28, 1922, was an American comic book writer, editor, publisher and producer famously known as Stan Lee. Lee was the Vice President in the Marvel Studios for decades, till his retirement in the 1990s. He passed away at November 12, 2018.

The Comics

Comics are visualized stories that combine images and text to tell a narrative, which are enjoyed by people of all ages and can be a fun way to explore stories and ideas. They usually consist of dialogues and thought in series of panels that show characters and actions, accompanied by in speech bubbles or captions. When Timely Comics was first created in the late 1930s, Goodman created the first few characters such as, the Human Torch and Namor, Captain America, X-men, Fantastic 4 and others.

The Multi-verse

The multi-verse is made up of countless different universes, each with its own version of characters and events. It is basically like an infinite number of parallel worlds, where each world has its own unique life. There are times that characters travel between these worlds and universes, leading to endless possibilities and a wide variety of events that occur.

The Gods

The Gods are the most powerful beings in the marvel universe. They are often thought as myths and legends in some worlds. The Asgardians, Elder Gods, the Olympians, and the Celestials are the Gods that often interact with the worlds and characters of the Marvel Universe. Their interactions with the characters brings rich and interesting layers to the events and stories.

The Avengers

Often in certain worlds in the multi-verse, there are certain superheroes who joins forces to protect their worlds together. The Avengers is one of the most powerful teams of superheroes who come together to defend the world from major threats. The team mainly consists of; Iron man, Captain America, Thor, Black widow, Hawkeye, Hulk, Wanda, Vision, Antman and Wasp. Together with the other superheroes, they protect the world from harm and danger as earth's mightiest heroes.

Symbols

Aside from the characters and their super powers, the Marvel Studios also use symbols in the stories and novels to enhance creativity and imagery. These gives the audience certain feelings and atmosphere when reading about or watching marvel stories and movies.
