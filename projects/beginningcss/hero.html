My Hero - Ironman

There was a man named Tony Stark, who was a chief weapons manufacturer for the U.S military who was cruel to certain people in the world. During that time, he encountered a serious incident that almost ended him. but fortunately recovered from it. From there on, he had a change of heart and use his great mind to create weaponized suits to use and protect the people of earth. He became what is called the Iron man, who is one of the iconic superheroes that protects earth in the marvel cinematic universe. He was the one who stopped the great war between Thanos the mad Titan and freed mankind.

HOME WORLD: earth

SUPERHERO TEAM: The Avenger

IRONMAN SUITS: weaponized suits made of titanium - gold alloys with the assistance of the artificial intelligence.
