About Ed Sheeran

Ed Sheeran is an English singer and songwriter born on February 17, 1991,in Halifax West Yorkshire, England. He is known for his wonderful lyrics and melodic acoustic sounds. Growing up in Framlingham, Suffolk, Sheeran developed a passion for music when he was young. As a teenager, he would learn guitar and write spactacular songs. In pursuing his career, he later moved to London in 2008 where he performed at certain places like the local gigs and theaters. His big hit came at 2011 when he released the album + (plus), featuring songs like "Lego House" and "The A Team". Since then, Sheeran has become one of the world's famous and best selling artists because of his top songs like "Shape of you", "Thinking Out Loud" and "Perfect".
