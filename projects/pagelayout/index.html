Traditions of Papua New Guineans

Papua New Guinea is one of the most diversed countries in the world today, With over 800 languages spoken. Having over 22 provinces, in four main regions the people of the nation still hold the traditions that has been passed down from their ancestors. These traditions are learnt and seen as the vital aspects of their lives.

Today the country has people from all over the world living in the towns and cities, whether teporary or permenant citizens. People are more involved with technology and other modern world influences that have huge impacts in their lives. However, the Papua New Guineans are still holding onto their beliefs and traditions as part of their lives.
