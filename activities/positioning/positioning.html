The world map is a visual representation of Earth's surface, showing continents, countries, oceans, and other geographical features. It helps people understand the layout of the planet and the relationships between different regions. World maps come in various forms, including political maps, which highlight country borders, and physical maps, which show mountains, rivers, and other natural features. Since the Earth is round, cartographers use different map projections to represent it on a flat surface, but each projection has some distortion. Maps are essential tools for navigation, education, and global communication, helping people explore and connect with the world.
