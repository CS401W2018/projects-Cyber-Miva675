  • The "action" attribute in the "form" tag specifies the location or web address where the data in the form will be sent to when the user submits.
  • The "method" attribute tells how the data is sent when the user submits the form. It is either sent through the "POST" value or the "GET" value.
  • A "POST" is when you send data online like pictures and personal information such as your personal pictures, names and emails. However, a "GET" is when you use or view data that are already in website, for instance, searching about something in google through the search bar.
