TAGS (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)

HEADER

The header usually contains the document's title, the meta data such as charsets and the external links to other scripts.

NAV

The NAV usually contain the links to a larger part of the page or sites, such as, another page in the same website. It is usually comes after the header at the top of the page. However, secondary navgations can be in the HEADER.

MAIN

The MAIN displays the main contents of the wb pages or sites. Usually the contents should not be repeated.

FOOTER

The footer basically contains copyright, authoship, contact information, related documents, site maps, back to top links and addresses.
